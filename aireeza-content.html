Services
I don't sell packages.
I solve problems.
Every founder I work with comes in with a different version of the same feeling: the business is running, but it's not right. We start there, not with a fixed methodology.
Who this is for
You're probably a good fit if...
You've been running your business for a while. Things are moving. But when you look at the numbers, or try to plan ahead, or think about where the money actually goes, something doesn't add up. You're not looking for someone to hand you a spreadsheet. You're looking for someone to help you think.
You're willing to look honestly at how your business is built, even if what you find isn't comfortable. The founders I work with best aren't afraid of the truth. They're just tired of not knowing it.
This probably isn't for you if...
You're looking for someone to validate what you already believe. Or you want a consultant who'll tell you everything is fine and hand you a branded deliverable. I'm not that. I'm the person who sits across from you and says the thing nobody else in the room will say.
I also don't work with businesses at idea stage. You need to have skin in the game already, real revenue, real problems, real stakes.
How it works
1
We start with an honest conversation
Not a discovery call with a pitch at the end. A real conversation about where your business is, what you're trying to build, and whether I can genuinely help. If I can't, I'll tell you that too.
2
I look before I recommend
I don't arrive with answers. I arrive with questions. I look at how your business actually makes and spends money, what's structural and what's behavioural, and where the real pressure is coming from. The diagnosis matters more than the prescription.
3
We build something that holds
The work isn't done when you feel better. It's done when your business runs better. That means structures you actually use, rhythms that don't fall apart when you're busy, and a clarity about your numbers that doesn't require me to be in the room.
4
You leave capable, not dependent
The goal of working with me is that eventually you don't need me. I want you to finish with a business you understand deeply and a set of tools you can use without me. That's what success looks like.
